About Cakedaddy

Our story of passion, quality, and delicious baked goods

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cakedaddy started as a small family bakery with a big dream: to bring joy to people's lives through delicious, freshly baked goods. What began as a humble neighborhood bakery has grown into a beloved destination for cake lovers and pastry enthusiasts alike.

Our founder, John Baker, started Cakedaddy with a simple philosophy: use only the finest ingredients, traditional baking methods, and a whole lot of love. Today, we continue to uphold these values while innovating with new flavors and designs that delight our customers.

Our Mission

At Cakedaddy, we believe that every celebration deserves a perfect cake, and every day deserves a sweet treat. We're committed to creating memorable experiences through our baked goods, whether it's a wedding cake that becomes the centerpiece of your special day or a simple cookie that brightens your afternoon.
